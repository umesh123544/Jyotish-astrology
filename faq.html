What is Vedic astrology and how is it different from Western astrology?

Vedic astrology, also known as Jyotish, is an ancient Indian system of astrology that dates back thousands of years. Unlike Western astrology which uses the tropical zodiac, Vedic astrology uses the sidereal zodiac which accounts for the precession of equinoxes. This makes Vedic astrology more accurate for predictive purposes.

Vedic astrology also places more emphasis on the Moon's position (Moon sign) rather than just the Sun sign, and uses a unique system of planetary periods (dashas) for timing events.

Can astrology predict exact dates of future events?

Vedic astrology can identify favorable and challenging periods with remarkable accuracy, but predicting exact dates is challenging because:

1. Human free will can alter outcomes
2. Collective karma affects individual timing
3. The precision depends on exact birth time accuracy

We typically provide time windows (like "between March-May 2025") rather than specific dates unless the astrological indicators are extremely strong.
